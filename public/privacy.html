Privacy Policy

Workspace Dashboard  ·  Last updated: July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Workspace Dashboard ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you connect your Google account.

1. What data we access

When you connect your Google account, we request access to the following Google APIs:

  • Gmail (read-only) — to display recent inbox messages in your dashboard.
  • Google Calendar (read-only) — to display your upcoming calendar events.
  • OpenID / email — to identify which Google account is connected.

2. How we use your data

We use Google account data solely to power your personal dashboard view:

  • Gmail messages are fetched and displayed in the dashboard. We do not read, store, or index email body content.
  • Calendar events are fetched and displayed so you can view your schedule. We do not store event details beyond what is shown on screen.
  • We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to displaying your own information back to you.

3. Data storage

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption before being stored in our database. We store only the minimum data required to maintain your connection. No email content or calendar event data is persisted to our database.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your Google account data with any third parties. Data fetched from Google APIs is used exclusively within your own session and dashboard.

5. Revoking access

You can disconnect your Google account at any time from within the app's settings. This immediately deletes your stored tokens. You can also revoke access directly at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

6. Data retention

Encrypted tokens are retained only while your account is connected. Upon disconnection or account deletion, all associated tokens are permanently removed from our systems.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including encrypted token storage and HTTPS-only communication. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: huzaifaarif@internetworks.io
